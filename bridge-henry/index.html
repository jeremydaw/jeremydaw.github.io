Jamison-Capital Bridge @ The Henry

Two bridge clubs in the Belconnen area of Canberra are joining forces to assist in the establishment of a social bridge activity at The Henry, Higgins, 94 Fullagar Cres, Higgins, ACT

This would be held on Mondays, 12:30 - 15:30 except public holidays, in the media rooom at The Henry.

We aim that it should commence on 21 August 2023 (but this may be subject to change).

Table fees are projected to be $5.00 cash or by token (5 tokens for $20)

We would play duplicate bridge, with a session director, using pre-dealt hands, with hand records available, and with scores calculated and published, but we won't be affiliated to the ABF or BFACT (so no masterpoints).
